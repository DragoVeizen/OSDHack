About the Hackathon

OSDHack 2019 is the Open Hackathon powered by the Open Source Developers Community (OSDC) based in and around JIIT, Noida. It is the 8-hour long hackathon where the participants will hack on various real-world problems being faced by the present world.

It is open to all tracks be it Machine Learning, Web Development, Android Development, AR/VR, Blockchain, Internet of Things etc. There is no boundation on the usage of technology.
